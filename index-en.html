About The Company

Nataj Advanced Trading Company was established in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and is one of the branches of Advanced Care for Commercial Business to be a strong foundation in the trade and transport sectors, especially for petrochemical and chemical materials, with a vision to expand its business in regional and global markets. The company combines experience and development to provide high-quality products and integrated and advanced solutions that meet the needs of multiple sectors, with a strict commitment to international standards of quality and safety.

Geographic Presence

The headquarters is located in the Sultanate of Oman, and the company has expanded its branches to Saudi Arabia, Egypt, and Turkey, covering a wide distribution network targeting regional and international markets.
